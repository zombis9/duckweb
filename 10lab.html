Mūsų Įgūdžiai

Diligently attending to the well-being of your ducks involves providing secure shelters for protection against weather and predators, along with a clean, dry retreat where they can rest, clean, and preen their feathers. Offering access to clean, germ-free water and a nutritionally balanced diet meets their hydration and nutritional needs. Recognizing the importance of adequate light stimulation, especially for layers, underscores your commitment to their overall health. Additionally, implementing strict disease prevention measures through vigilant observation and routine health checks demonstrates your dedication to ensuring a safe and thriving environment for your ducks.
